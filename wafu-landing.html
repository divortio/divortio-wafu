Security Model

A Zero Trust Architecture for Your Origins.

WAFu's primary goal is to make your origin servers invisible to the public internet. Attackers can't bypass WAFu because there's no direct path to your app.

For Public Origins (e.g. Cloudflare Pages)

Authenticated Origin Pulls (mTLS)

A secure tunnel requires a secret client certificate from Cloudflare. Your origin drops any connection that doesn't have it.

For Private Origins (e.g. Workers)

Service Bindings

Your origin worker has no public URL. WAFu is given a private, internal path to communicate with it that no one else can use.
